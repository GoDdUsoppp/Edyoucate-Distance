Common questions answered

1. Why is EDYOUCATE the Best Distance Education Consultant in Surat?
EDYOUCATE is one of the leading education consultancies in Surat, helping students and working professionals choose the right UGC-DEB approved online degree programs. We provide free career counselling, university comparison, admission assistance, scholarship guidance, and complete support until your admission is confirmed.
2. Is an Online Degree from UGC-DEB Approved Universities Valid?
Yes. Degrees offered by UGC-DEB approved universities are recognized across India and are generally accepted for private sector jobs, higher education, competitive examinations, and government opportunities where the university and course meet the applicable eligibility requirements.
3. Can I Study While Working Full-Time?
Absolutely! Online distance education is designed for working professionals. You can attend live or recorded lectures, access study materials anytime, and complete your degree without leaving your job.
4. Which Courses Are Available Through Online Distance Education?
We help students enroll in a wide range of undergraduate and postgraduate programs, including Online MBA, BBA, BCA, B.Com, BA, MA, M.Com, MCA, MSc, Diploma, and Certificate courses from India's top universities.
5. How Do I Choose the Right University?
Our expert counsellors compare universities based on recognition, accreditation, course structure, fees, placement support, and your career goals. We recommend the best university according to your academic background and future plans.
6. Are Online Degrees Accepted for Government Jobs?
Degrees from recognized UGC-DEB approved universities may be accepted for government recruitment where they satisfy the eligibility criteria specified by the recruiting authority. Always check the notification for the specific job you are applying for.
7. What Makes EDYOUCATE the Best Online Distance Education Consultant in Surat?
EDYOUCATE offers personalized career counselling, expert admission guidance, affordable fee options, scholarship assistance, university comparison, document verification, and end-to-end support—making the admission process simple and hassle-free.
8. How Much Does an Online Degree Cost?
The fee depends on the university and course you choose. We help you find affordable online degree programs with scholarship opportunities and EMI options to suit your budget.
9. How Can I Get Free Career Counselling?
You can book a free one-to-one counselling session with our education experts. We'll understand your goals, recommend the right course and university, and guide you through the complete admission process.
10. Why Should I Choose Online Distance Education?
Online distance education offers flexibility, affordability, recognized degrees, industry-relevant curriculum, and the convenience to study from anywhere while balancing your job, business, or personal commitments.
