About edYOUcate

Surat's most trusted online education consultancy

Who we are, what we believe in, and why 500+ students trust us

edYOUcate is a Surat-based education consultancy founded with one simple belief — that every student and working professional deserves access to quality higher education, no matter their schedule, budget, or background.

We saw too many talented people in Gujarat give up on their education dreams because traditional colleges didn't fit their lives. Full-time attendance, high fees, rigid schedules — these were barriers that shouldn't exist in today's world.

So we built edYOUcate — a consultancy that puts students first. We don't just help you enroll; we understand your life situation, recommend the right program honestly, handle all the paperwork, and support you long after admission day.

FAQ

Common questions answered

Is an online degree valid for government jobs in India?
Yes. Degrees from UGC-DEB approved universities are fully recognized by the Government of India and accepted for government jobs, private sector employment, and higher studies like MBA or civil services.
Can I study while working a full-time job?
Absolutely. Online programs are specifically designed for working professionals. You can study at any time — morning, evening, or weekends — completely at your own pace with recorded lectures available 24/7.
Is counseling at edYOUcate really free?
Yes, completely free with no obligation. Our counselors guide you to the best program honestly — we earn only when you successfully enroll, so our advice is always in your best interest.
